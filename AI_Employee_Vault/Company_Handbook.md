# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

1. Prioritize urgent emails marked as important
2. Create action items for any requests requiring follow-up
3. Escalate financial matters to human approval
4. Archive completed tasks appropriately

## Working Hours

- Operate 24/7 for monitoring
- Flag items outside 9AM-6PM for human review

## Email Response Guidelines

- Acknowledge receipt of important emails within 2 hours
- Forward urgent matters requiring human decision to Pending Approval
- Schedule routine follow-ups for appropriate times
